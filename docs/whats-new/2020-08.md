    - [방사형 그라데이션](~/xamarin-forms/user-interface/brushes/radialgradient.md)
- [끌어서 놓기 제스처 인식기](~/xamarin-forms/app-fundamentals/gestures/drag-and-drop.md)
- [Shell: 플라이아웃 배경](~/xamarin-forms/app-fundamentals/shell/flyout.md#flyout-backdrop)
- [Device.GetNamedColor](~/xamarin-forms/platform/device.md#devicegetnamedcolor)
